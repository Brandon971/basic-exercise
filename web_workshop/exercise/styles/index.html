About Atti

THE ATTI EXPERIENCE CENTRE IS WHERE CUSTOMERS CAN OPT TO COLLECT THEIR PURCHASE FROM IF THEY ARE BASED IN CAPE TOWN AND WANT THE SENSE OF AN UNIQUE CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE. THE EXPERIENCE CENTRE (EC) IS NOT OPEN TO THE PUBLIC, AND IS ONLY OPEN TO CUSTOMERS WHO DECIDE TO COLLECT THEIR ITEM IN CAPE TOWN. AT THE EXPERIENCE CENTRE, CUSTOMERS CAN GET THEIR SNEAKERS CLEANED, CHILL OUT IN THE LOUNGE AREA WITH FREE WIFI, ENJOY A GAME OF PLAYSTATION, AND GET TREATED TO DIFFERENT TYPES OF CEREAL. BEFORE LEAVING THE EC CUSTOMERS CAN TAKE A PHOTO AT THE ATTI MURAL DONE BY A WELL KNOWN LOCAL GRAFFITI ARTIST.
